Artificial Teeth/Complete Dentures

Dentures are removable false teeth made of acrylic (plastic), nylon or metal. They fit snugly over the gums to replace missing teeth and eliminate potential problems caused by gaps.

Dental Implants are used as a platform to replace your missing tooth by a bio-compatible material. Dental Implants have become an integral part of tooth replacement

Extractions

Tooth extraction is the forced removal of a tooth, using forceps, due to various reasons such as: tooth decay,tooth damage extremely loose tooth

Fixed Orthodontic Treatment

Orthodontics is the branch of dentistry that corrects teeth and jaws that are positioned improperly. Crooked teeth and teeth that do not fit together correctly are harder to keep clean
